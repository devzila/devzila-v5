Ruby on Rails Upgrades

Keeping your Rails app up-to-date is crucial for security and performance. We handle seamless migrations to the latest Ruby and Rails versions, ensuring minimal downtime and maximum reliability.

Plan my upgrade → What we do

What we do

We de-risk upgrades by moving in safe, incremental steps — backed by tests and a clear rollback plan — so your team keeps shipping while we modernize the foundation.

⬆️

Version Migration

Step-by-step upgrades to the latest Ruby and Rails, following the official upgrade path safely.

🔒

Security Patching

Eliminate known vulnerabilities in Ruby, Rails, and your gem dependencies.

📦

Dependency Upgrades

Audit and modernize outdated gems, replacing abandoned libraries with maintained alternatives.

🟢

Zero-Downtime Deploys

Roll out upgrades behind feature flags and dual-boot strategies to avoid disruption.

🧪

CI Safety Net

Strengthen your test suite and CI so every step of the upgrade is verified automatically.

📈

Performance Gains

Take advantage of new framework features and runtime improvements as you upgrade.

What you get

  • A clear, prioritized upgrade roadmap with effort estimates.
  • Incremental, reviewable pull requests — never a risky big-bang rewrite.
  • Up-to-date dependencies free of known CVEs.
  • A faster, more secure, and more maintainable application.
